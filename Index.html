At WHRPG we drive dialogue through data driven research policy and advocacy. We work to advocate by conducting policy research, educating voters, and informing legislators on our focus issues. These include Civic Engagement, Economic Development, Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services, as well as Technology & Innovation. With your help we want to make our Arizona a better place.

We hope that you find our first paper insightful and useful in the fight to protect our public schools.
