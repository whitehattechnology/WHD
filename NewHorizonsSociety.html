New Horizons Society

The fight for democracy and defense of our shared values is long and full of uncertainty. We are David to the Goliath.  We are determined to be successful, but we need your help. Members of the New Horizons Society are the founding financial supporters; they think strategically and excersise patience.  Your funds will support the following;

* Research Papers (AZ Specific)
* WHRPG Operations (Staff & Facilities)
* Outreach to Legislators
* Fellowship Sponsorship
* Special Events

At WHRPG we center our cause on the common good of every American. We value your support.  Contact Executive Director Damian Preciado at damianpreciado@whrpg.org for more information.
