Our Mission

White Hat Democrats began as a grassroots organization. It's initial focus, to inform the average citizen of their ability to engage with their party in the political process. Informally founded in 2016, the organization has matured and expanded, we now formally operate as non-profit/C-4. 

In this role, we seek to drive meaningful dialogue through the dissemination of data driven information analysis. As we face the continual erosion of Democratic institutions, WHD looks to stem the tide by conducting policy research, educating voters, and informing legislators on our focus issues.  These include Civic Engagement, Economic Development, Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services, as well as Technology & Innovation. We are based in Arizona and with your help, we will make a positive impact across our entire country.
