Initially founded in 2016 as a grass roots organization, White Hat Research & Policy Group came together through the spirit of civic engagement.  Our organization is bipartisan and operates as Arizona's progressive think tank.

WHRPG drives meaningful dialogue through data driven research policy and advocacy. As we face the erosion of our democratic institutions, WHRPG works to protect them by conducting policy research, educating voters, and informing legislators on our focus issues. These include Civic Engagement, Economic Development, Education, Criminal Justice, Human Services, as well as Technology & Innovation. With your help we want to make our state a better place.
