WHRPG Fellowship

Creative Fellow

The White Hat Research and Policy Group offers a unique opportunity to the artistic community. The Creative Fellowship program aims to empower students and early career professionals working in artistic mediums such as creative writing, graphic arts, drama, dance, etc.   This work is meant to enrich the public policy dialogue through creative exploration of the issues.

As a Fellow you will report to the Creative Director who will guide the alignment of the research with the artistic interpretation.  Each Creative Fellowship grant will fund a specific artistic project that adapts our research on a particular issue for a wide audience.
